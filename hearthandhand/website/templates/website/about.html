The Foundation

Judy founded Hearth & Hand after 35 years of managing spaces that mattered—restaurants, medical offices, family homes, community hubs. She learned that great service isn't transactional. It's relational.

A Different Kind of Leadership

As an ADHD Latina who's run teams, navigated bureaucracy, and juggled multiple cities, she knows what it's like to need systems that just work. No extra noise. No wasted steps. Just calm, competent execution.

That philosophy shaped everything: how we hire, how we train, how we show up for clients.

From Hospitality to Home

After years of running Mason's Famous Lobster Rolls in downtown Charleston—managing daily service, building teams, and learning what consistency actually means—she decided to build something more intimate and intentional.

Hearth & Hand was born from one idea: if you treat someone's home with the same care you'd give a thriving restaurant, everything changes.
