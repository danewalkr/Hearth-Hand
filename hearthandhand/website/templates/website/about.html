The Foundation

Judy founded Hearth & Hand after 35 years in service: restaurants, medical offices, family businesses, community spaces. She learned early that great service isn't about transactions. It's about relationships.

A Different Kind of Leadership

As an ADHD Latina who's managed teams, navigated complex systems, and split time between cities, Judy knows what it means to need support that actually works. No wasted energy. No complicated systems. Just calm, reliable execution.

That's the philosophy that built Hearth & Hand. It shapes how we hire, how we train, how we show up for every client.

From Restaurant to Home

Judy ran Mason's Famous Lobster Rolls in downtown Charleston for years. She managed daily service, built teams, and learned what consistency really means. Then she decided to build something more personal and intentional.

Hearth & Hand came from one belief: if you treat someone's home with the same care you'd give a great restaurant, everything changes.
